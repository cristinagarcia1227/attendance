Class Attendance System
Smart Attendance Management for Teachers
👋 Welcome! Here's how to get started:
1
⚙️ Setup your class info
Go to Setup tab → enter your Subject, Teacher name, and School Year.
2
📚 Add your sections
In Setup → My Sections, rename Section 1 to your actual section (e.g. Sampaguita). Add more sections if needed.
3
👥 Add your students
Go to Roster tab → add students one by one, or upload a CSV file with names, parent contacts, and section.
4
📋 Take attendance!
Go to Attendance tab → select your section → tap each student to mark Present, Late, or Absent.
💡
Your attendance data is saved automatically in your browser. SMS features require internet, but all attendance marking and records work anytime.
📖 Tab Guide
📋 Attendance
Select a section → tap student cards to mark Present ✔, Late ⏰, or Absent ✘. Has Undo button for mistakes.
👥 Roster
Manage your student list. Add names, parent names, phone numbers, gender, and section. Upload via CSV or type manually.
💬 Messages
Auto-generates parent messages. Send via 📱 Prepaid SMS (your SIM load) or 💬 Viber. Notify all absent/late at once.
📊 Log
Full attendance history. Filter by section or date. Export to CSV or SF2 format for DepEd records.
🏫 Overview
See all sections at a glance — how many present, late, absent, and unmarked per section today.
📱 QR Code
Print and post in your classroom. Scan it to open the app quickly on your phone.
⚙️ Setup
Class info, section names, Semaphore API for auto-SMS, backup/sync between devices, and reset option.
💡 Tips & Reminders
💾 Your data auto-saves
No need to click Save — attendance is saved automatically as you tap. But always use Export Backup regularly just in case!
📲 Sync between phone & laptop
Setup → Backup & Sync → Export Full Backup → send via Viber to other device → Import Backup.
↩ Made a mistake?
After marking a student, a black Undo bar appears at the bottom for 6 seconds. Tap it to reverse the mark before SMS sends.
📩 SMS options
Prepaid: Free to set up, uses your SIM load (~₱1/SMS). Works offline.
Semaphore: Auto-sends in background, ₱0.50/SMS, needs internet. Only sends for Absent & Late.
🗂 SF2 Export
Log tab → Export for SF2 → downloads a JSON file. Run with the sf2_export.py script on your PC to auto-fill your SF2 Excel file.
⚠️ Don't clear browser data
Your student roster and attendance are stored in Chrome's local storage. Clearing browser data or cache will erase everything. Always export a backup first!
